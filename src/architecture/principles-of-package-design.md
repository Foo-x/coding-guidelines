- [Common Reuse Principle (全再利用の原則)](CRP.md)
- [Common Closure Principle (閉鎖性共通の原則)](CCP.md)
- [Acyclic Dependencies Principle (非循環依存関係の原則)](ADP.md)
- [Stable Dependencies Principle (安定依存の原則)](SDP.md)
- [Stable Abstractions Principle (安定度・抽象度等価の原則)](SAP.md)
